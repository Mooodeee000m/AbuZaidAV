Success Stories

Stadium Network Redesign for Enhanced Fan Experience

Revamping the entire network infrastructure of a major sports stadium to support high-density Wi-Fi, digital signage, and broadcast media.

Challenge:

Existing network couldn't handle thousands of concurrent users, leading to poor connectivity and a fragmented fan experience.

Architecture:

Designed a robust, segmented network with high-capacity wireless access points, a dedicated fiber backbone, and a redundant core, ensuring pervasive and reliable connectivity.

Tools/Vendors:

Cisco Catalyst, Meraki Wi-Fi, Aruba ClearPass, Fortinet Firewalls.

Measurable Outcome:

90% increase in concurrent Wi-Fi users, 85% reduction in network-related fan complaints, and seamless integration of new digital displays.

Theater AV over IP: Immersive Audiovisual Experience

Implementing a state-of-the-art AV over IP system for a new performing arts theater, ensuring flexible content distribution and superior audio-visual quality.

Challenge:

Traditional AV cabling was restrictive and costly for a multi-stage, multi-zone venue requiring dynamic content routing.

Architecture:

Deployed an SDVoE-based AV over IP solution over a dedicated 10GbE network, allowing any source to be routed to any display or speaker zone with zero latency.

Tools/Vendors:

ZeeVee ZyPer4K, Extron, Q-SYS, Shure Microphones.

Measurable Outcome:

Simplified content management, reduced installation costs by 30%, and provided unparalleled flexibility for dynamic events and performances.

Enterprise Multicast Issue Resolution & Optimization

Diagnosing and resolving complex multicast routing issues for a financial trading firm, ensuring reliable data feed distribution.

Challenge:

Intermittent packet loss and latency in critical multicast trading data feeds, leading to financial impact and operational disruptions.

Architecture:

Conducted deep packet inspection and configuration audits across the core network. Re-architected PIM and IGMP configurations, implementing a robust multicast design for resilience.

Tools/Vendors:

Wireshark, Cisco IOS/NX-OS, Riverbed NetProfiler.

Measurable Outcome:

Eliminated multicast packet loss, achieved consistent sub-millisecond latency for critical data feeds, and restored full operational confidence.

QoS Redesign for High-Quality VoIP Environment

Optimizing Quality of Service (QoS) for a large enterprise with a heavily utilized Voice over IP (VoIP) system experiencing call quality issues.

Challenge:

Poor voice quality, dropped calls, and jitter affecting over 2,000 users across multiple locations due to network congestion.

Architecture:

Implemented DiffServ (DSCP) marking, strict priority queuing on WAN interfaces, and bandwidth shaping policies. Configured proper classification and policing at network edges.

Tools/Vendors:

Cisco Routers/Switches, UC Monitoring Tools.

Measurable Outcome:

95% improvement in Mean Opinion Score (MOS) for VoIP calls, eliminated dropped calls, and significantly enhanced user satisfaction and productivity.
