Frequently Asked Questions

What are your typical response times?

For email inquiries, we aim to respond within 24 business hours. For critical support requests from existing clients, response times are defined by your service level agreement (SLA).

Do you offer remote delivery of services?

Yes, absolutely. Our methodology is designed for efficient and secure remote delivery of network consulting, audits, and design services, allowing us to serve clients globally.

Can you sign an NDA?

Yes, we are fully prepared to sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) to protect your sensitive information and project details. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of our practice.

What is your pricing model?

Our pricing is typically project-based, tailored to the scope and complexity of the services required. For ongoing support or specific consultations, we can arrange hourly rates or retainer agreements. We'll provide a clear proposal after an initial discovery phase.
