Our Impact: Mini Case Studies

Enterprise Multicast Optimization

Challenge:

A large enterprise faced intermittent video conferencing issues and poor IPTV performance across its distributed campus network.

Solution:

Implemented a comprehensive multicast design, optimized IGMP snooping, and configured PIM sparse mode across core switches, significantly reducing network traffic load.

Outcome:

99% reduction in video conferencing latency and stable, high-quality IPTV streams, improving internal communication and operational efficiency.

QoS Redesign for VoIP Excellence

Challenge:

A growing call center experienced dropped calls and poor voice quality, impacting customer service and agent productivity.

Solution:

Redesigned their Quality of Service (QoS) policies, implementing strict priority queueing for VoIP traffic and bandwidth reservations for critical applications.

Outcome:

Near-perfect voice call clarity and reliability, leading to increased customer satisfaction scores and a 15% improvement in agent handling time.

Data Center Network Segmentation

Challenge:

A financial institution needed to enhance security and compliance within its data center, with strict requirements for server-to-server communication control.

Solution:

Designed and deployed a granular VLAN segmentation strategy, integrating with existing firewalls and implementing access control lists (ACLs) at key aggregation points.

Outcome:

Achieved full compliance with industry regulations, significantly improved security posture, and reduced the blast radius of potential security breaches.
